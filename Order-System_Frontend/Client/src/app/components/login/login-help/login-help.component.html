Help: Login

If you're unsure how log into the system, please follow the steps mentioned below

  • Step 1: Enter in your username that you register with.
  • Step 2: Enter in your password that you register with.
  • Step 3: Login, by clicking the "Login" button

    • *Note: Ensure that all fields are filled in correctly, before attempting to save the item

If you forgot your password on the system, please follow the steps mentioned below

  • Step 1: If you forgot your password, click "Forgot password" button
  • Step 2: Enter in your email address that you register with.
  • Step 3: Receive a reset email, by clicking the "Send" button

    • *Note: Ensure that all fields are filled in correctly, before attempting to save the item
